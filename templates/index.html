Proof of Concept:

This test image serves as a proof of concept (POC) and a case study to demonstrate the authentication process. It simulates an image that a creator has uploaded, and you, as the consumer, are now trying to authenticate it. Here's how the process works:

  • Content Creation and Hashing: The image was first processed through our content creator's hub (currently in production), where it was hashed using our advanced Hash Feature. This hash was then recorded on the blockchain via a transaction, ensuring an immutable record of the image's integrity.
  • 2. Download the Test Image: The provided test image simulates a piece of digital content you want to verify.
  • 3. Upload the Test Image: Upload the downloaded image to the authentication system.
  • 4. Verification Process: The system will generate a hash of the uploaded image and check this hash against the records in our test wallet on the blockchain. It ensures the content matches the original stored hash.

This POC demonstrates how a creator's image is hashed and recorded on the blockchain, and how the consumer can verify its authenticity using our system.
